  • Sand

    The Island has a variety of sand leagues available for men, women and coed, as well. There are options of doubles, fours and sixes. The leagues take place on several different days of the week so everyone can find a time that works for them.

  • Vacations

    In addition to leagues, the Island organizes 3 major trips South of the Border every year. Here you are able to enjoy the fun and sun while you play lots of volleyball all week. The resorts are all inclusive and the volleyball is plentiful.

  • Hardcourt & Grass

    Not only do we have sand leagues, but we have indoor leagues that are both coed and women's sixes. Additionally, we have lots and lots of grass volleyball. We have 8 different divisions on 5 different nights located at parks all around Denver.
